# Non-Urgent Use

OpenCareLoop is for slow, careful organization of health context. It is not for emergencies, sudden severe symptoms, or situations where waiting could be dangerous.

Use it for:

- Preparing for a scheduled appointment.
- Tracking a recurring or long-running symptom.
- Organizing older records.
- Comparing what changed before and after a treatment, visit, or test.
- Drafting questions to ask a clinician.

Do not use it as the normal workflow when the situation may be urgent. If a symptom is sudden, severe, rapidly worsening, or feels dangerous, stop using the normal OpenCareLoop workflow and seek appropriate medical help.

## Keep decisions in the right place

The dossier can help you ask better questions. It should not turn AI output into medical orders.

Do not start, stop, combine, or change the dose or timing of prescription medicines based only on dossier notes or AI suggestions. Put those ideas into the questions-for-doctor list unless a qualified clinician has already given that instruction.

The same caution applies to risky changes involving supplements, fasting, procedures, pregnancy-related decisions, intense exercise, or delaying care.
